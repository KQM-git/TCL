**Notes**

* **Windwheel Roll** and **Whirlwind Kick Damage** aren't blunt and thus won't shatter.
* **Windwheel Roll** and **Whirlwind Kick Damage** snapshot.

* **Windwheel Roll** has a 3s ICD for energy generation.
