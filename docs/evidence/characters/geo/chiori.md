## Skill Mechanics
* Chiori can perform a plunge attack regardless of the dash height after using hE: [YouTube](https://youtu.be/68eybo5fdJc) - @soul_fish
  * This does not bypass height requirements for plunges - if the area is vertically restricted, plunging is unavailable unlike other characters with similar mechanics: [YouTube](https://youtu.be/86e4O2EOwwA) - @nickjr
* Chiori's Skill allows swapping in mid-air: [YouTube](https://youtu.be/yq8OH9KKlH0) - @.magnusartifex
* Chiori's skill spawns the additional Tamoto based on the Tamato's proximity to a geo construct: [YouTube](https://youtu.be/qE6qKzCKUqI) - @soul_fish
* Each Chiori can have 2 of their own Tamoto dolls in co-op: [YouTube](https://youtu.be/felcp1dULx0) - @soul_fish
* Certain enemy constructs count as a construct for Chiori's Skill: [Geo Samachurl](https://youtu.be/vD9p_SEnMTs), [Geo Hypostasis, and Cryo Samachurl](https://youtu.be/uDVz89LnMr0) - @laylaithink
* Swapping Chiori off-field manually during her Tailor-Made talent window will cause neither effect to occur: [YouTube](https://youtu.be/4MpaQVHsfnY) - @

* Tamato has three possible expressions, with a ~70% chance for it being closed eyes and ~15% for the others: [Google Sheets](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/e/2PACX-1vTckqtHp4qER45qW1pELulz7QzpydMjjWLPk47MlmwbvRjKAT51ntEOqPQ-7Wz_AWfAb1T8BgLVDx_O/pubhtml#) - @soul_fish
